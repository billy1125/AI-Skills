## Introduction

### 結構（IMRaD 模型的 I）
典型三段式結構：
1. **Hook / Background**：建立研究領域的重要性
2. **Research Gap**：指出現有文獻的不足
3. **Purpose Statement**：說明本研究的目的與範圍

### 常用句型

**建立背景：**
- `Over the past decade, X has emerged as a central concern in the field of Y.`
- `The rapid proliferation of X has prompted growing interest in Y.`
- `X has long been recognized as a significant factor in Y.`

**指出研究缺口：**
- `However, relatively little attention has been paid to...`
- `Despite considerable progress, the mechanisms underlying X remain poorly understood.`
- `Existing studies have predominantly focused on X, leaving Y largely unexplored.`
- `Prior research has yielded inconsistent findings regarding...`

**說明研究目的：**
- `The present study aims to investigate / examine / explore...`
- `This paper seeks to address this gap by...`
- `Drawing on [theory/method], this study examines...`

### 注意事項
- 避免過於寬泛的開場（"Since the beginning of human history..."）
- Research gap 須有文獻支撐，不能只靠斷言
- Purpose statement 應明確（what, how, why）

## Literature Review

### 結構
組織方式可選：
- **主題式**（Thematic）：按議題分組討論文獻
- **時間式**（Chronological）：按發展脈絡梳理
- **方法式**（Methodological）：按研究方法分類

### 常用句型

**引介文獻：**
- `Smith (2020) argues that...`
- `A number of studies have demonstrated that... (Jones, 2019; Lee & Park, 2021).`
- `Building on the work of X, Y proposes that...`

**比較與對比：**
- `While X contends that..., Y maintains that...`
- `In contrast to X's findings, Y reports that...`
- `These results are consistent with / contradict those of...`

**批判整合：**
- `However, these studies share a common limitation: they fail to account for...`
- `Although influential, this framework has been criticized for its overemphasis on...`
- `The evidence, though suggestive, remains largely correlational.`

**銜接到本研究：**
- `The present study extends this line of inquiry by...`
- `To address the aforementioned gaps, this study adopts...`

### 注意事項
- **避免純描述性堆砌**（A says... B says... C says...），需有批判整合
- 每段應有明確的主題，聚焦一個子議題
- 結尾段落應橋接到本研究的理論框架或研究問題

## Methodology

### 結構
1. Research Design（研究設計）
2. Participants / Data Sources（研究對象/資料來源）
3. Instruments / Measures（研究工具/測量）
4. Procedure（研究程序）
5. Data Analysis（資料分析）

### 語態慣例
- 量化研究：多用**被動語態**（Data were collected... Participants were randomly assigned...）
- 質性研究：可用**主動語態**（We conducted semi-structured interviews...）

### 常用句型

**研究設計：**
- `This study employed a [cross-sectional / longitudinal / mixed-methods] design to...`
- `A quasi-experimental approach was adopted to...`
- `Data were collected through [surveys / interviews / archival records].`

**研究對象：**
- `Participants were recruited from [setting] using [purposive / random / snowball] sampling.`
- `The final sample consisted of N participants (n female, n male), aged X to Y years (M = , SD = ).`
- `Inclusion criteria required that participants...`

**測量工具：**
- `X was measured using the [Scale Name] developed by Y (year), which comprises N items rated on a N-point Likert scale.`
- `The internal consistency of the scale in the current sample was acceptable (α = .XX).`

**資料分析：**
- `Data were analyzed using [software] version X.`
- `Descriptive statistics were computed for all variables.`
- `Multiple regression analysis was conducted to examine the relationship between...`
- `Thematic analysis (Braun & Clarke, 2006) was used to identify recurring patterns across interviews.`

## Results

### 原則
- **只呈現發現，不詮釋意義**（詮釋留給 Discussion）
- 圖表說明不應與正文完全重複
- 按研究問題的順序呈現結果

### 常用句型

**描述統計：**
- `Descriptive statistics for all study variables are presented in Table 1.`
- `The mean score for X was M = XX (SD = XX).`

**推論統計：**
- `Results indicated a significant positive relationship between X and Y (r = .XX, p < .001).`
- `The regression model was statistically significant (F(df) = XX, p < .001), accounting for XX% of the variance in Y.`
- `No significant difference was found between groups (t(df) = XX, p = .XX).`

**質性結果：**
- `Three major themes emerged from the analysis: (1)..., (2)..., and (3)...`
- `Participants consistently reported that...`
- `Representative quotes illustrating this theme include: "..." (Participant 3).`

### 注意事項
- 時態：通常用**過去式**報告已完成的分析
- 避免說 "proves" → 改用 `suggests`, `indicates`, `demonstrates`

## Discussion

### 結構
1. 摘要主要發現（brief recap）
2. 詮釋每個發現，連結現有文獻
3. 說明理論貢獻與實務意涵
4. 承認研究限制
5. 提出未來研究方向

### 常用句型

**回顧發現：**
- `The present study set out to examine... The findings reveal that...`
- `Consistent with the hypothesis, results showed that...`
- `Contrary to expectations, no significant effect of X on Y was observed.`

**連結文獻：**
- `This finding aligns with / corroborates the work of X, who reported...`
- `These results extend X's framework by demonstrating that...`
- `The discrepancy between the current findings and those of X may be attributable to...`

**說明意涵：**
- `These findings have important implications for [theory / practice / policy].`
- `The results suggest that practitioners should consider...`

**說明限制：**
- `Several limitations of this study warrant acknowledgment.`
- `The cross-sectional design precludes causal inferences.`
- `The sample was drawn from a single institution, which may limit the generalizability of findings.`

**未來研究：**
- `Future research should examine X using longitudinal designs.`
- `Replication with more diverse samples would strengthen confidence in these findings.`

## Conclusion

### 原則
- **不引入新論點**
- 簡短扼要（通常 1–3 段）
- 強調研究貢獻，呼應 Introduction 提出的研究缺口

### 常用句型
- `In conclusion, this study has demonstrated that...`
- `The present research contributes to the literature by...`
- `Taken together, these findings underscore the importance of...`
- `In sum, the evidence presented here suggests that...`

## Abstract

### 結構（約 150–300 字，視期刊規定）
1. Background / Purpose（1–2 句）
2. Methods（2–3 句）
3. Results（2–3 句）
4. Conclusions / Implications（1–2 句）

### 注意事項
- 全文使用**過去式**（研究已完成）
- 不引用文獻
- 避免縮寫（除非極常見）
- 關鍵詞（Keywords）另列，通常 5 個，按字母排序

### 範本框架
```
This study investigated [topic] among [sample] in [context].
[N] participants completed [measures/procedures].
[Statistical/analytical approach] was used to [purpose].
Results indicated that [key finding 1] and [key finding 2].
These findings suggest [implication], highlighting the need for [future direction].
```
